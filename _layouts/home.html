MISSION

Holding Nature Activities and Social Events to Support Worthy Environmental/Human Development Groups While Building Consciousness

JAMBO's Goals

To raise funds for reliable organizations which are serving both natural preservation / restoration and human development

To provide activities, especially those which give people more exposure to and appreciation for the natural environment, to raise funds or to volunteer for environmental (and/or human) betterment

To serve as a liaison between the do-good organizations JAMBO supports and common folk, providing opportunities for them to meet and get more involved with each other.

The JAMBO program raises funds for exceptional organizations at the same time as getting people into nature and/or in direct touch with these organizations. This is done with the hope that participants will come to more greatly appreciate the natural environment and the good groups which we support and get more actively involved in supporting environmental protection however they can.

Why donate?

There are 3 good reasons why people want to support JAMBO International Inc.

1.

JAMBO researches organizations and gives to those which meet the following criteria:

  • Strive to work towards simultaneously bettering both the natural environment and the social environment (especially empowering the “disadvantaged”), with the belief that these 2 issues cannot be dealt with separately if we wish to move towards a sustainable world
  • Have a high degree of transparency and financial integrity (understood through personal visitations or because of receiving high marks by independent analysts like Charity Navigator)

2.

JAMBO International Inc is inspired and is being helped by a very successful model - JAMBO International Japan. JAMBO Japan has been active since 1996, holding regular activities which get people to get in touch with nature (mainly through hikes), do environmental projects or volunteer for the social good. It raises funds through its projects. In this way, it supports NPOs while getting people to actively work in and more greatly appreciate the natural environment. By getting into nature and appreciating its wonders, people come to love it more, and it is only in the process of that we come to deeply treasure and devote our energy to its preservation. Many of the funds raised herein would not be coming in without JAMBO’s efforts.

3.

In conjunction with JAMBO Japan, JAMBO International Inc is working to develop JAMBO groups around the US (and throughout the world) that are based on a similar model.
